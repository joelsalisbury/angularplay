How to Play

Following the rules of Latin poetic scansion, mark each syllable as long ( - ) or short ( u ).

Each correct response earns 6 points. Unlock point multipliers with streaks of 10 or more correct responses in a row.

Game Modes

Marathon - an entire selection, no time limit
Timed - 2, 5, or 10 minute limit, lines can be randomized
